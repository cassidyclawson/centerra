Committed to Growers

Centerra Capital has deep roots in the agriculture sector. Our founder grew up on a farm and many of our first clients were family growers and food processors in the Central Valley. We understand the unique needs facing your sector.

ADVISORY & FINANCE

Over the last 17 years we’ve expanded the reach of our practice and developed strong relationships with regional and global capital providers. This enables us to bring the tools of modern finance to rural businesses. Our clients call us when they want to make big things happen, like increasing acreage under production or expanding into new verticals.

ISSUES OF FAMILY OWNERSHIP

The Central Valley was built on the shoulders of rural family businesses. For owners of these closely-held entities, the health of their business is often deeply connected to the well-being of their family. Business decisions should be considered with both organizational and personal outcomes in mind.

Centerra Capital has decades of experience navigating this complex landscape, and can help you grow your business, protect personal wealth, and plan for a successful succession of ownership.

AGTECH

Over the last fifty years, advances in data science, chemistry, genetics and logistics have radically transformed our food system. We believe the pace of innovation will continue to accelerate. High net-worth individuals and financial institutions in the Central Valley are uniquely qualified to participate in the coming boom.
