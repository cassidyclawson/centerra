Real Estate Advisory

Centerra Capital helps businesses, investors and developers maximize returns with real estate. We have strong relationships with lenders and offer a comprehensive suite of services, including market research, due diligence, valuations and brokerage. Clients depend on our disciplined processes and regional expertise to surface and execute successful real estate transactions of any type and scope.

ACQUISITIONS

Whether you’re considering the acquisition of a single asset or a portfolio of properties, Centerra Capital provides you with dependable investor intelligence, sophisticated deal structuring and access to capital. Our expertise spans all asset types, including residential, agricultural and commercial properties for income or owner-occupation. We can help you identify property or you can feed us listings for consideration.

Our market intelligence is unmatched in the Central Valley and includes data on transactions, rents, demographics and tax/title/zoning. This data complements our traditional tools of analysis: assessment of physical assets, inspection of title, and review of lease history. We ensure that you enter every potential transaction with a comprehensive understanding of risks, returns and potential outcomes.

We carefully manage the acquisition lifecycle and apply appropriate due diligence at every stage. Our team ensures that your strategy maximizes value, minimizes costs and optimizes tax implications on both a property and entity basis.

DISPOSITION

Centerra Capital helps you maximize value when selling your assets. We can manage the disposition of individual properties or engineer more complex portfolio sales to institutional clients and private equity firms.

Our longstanding regional presence enables us to introduce your assets to qualified buyers, and our strong reputation ensures we communicate its value with credibility.

REPOSITIONING

Centerra Capital empowers investors and developers to execute ambitious repositioning projects. We have stewarded many successful programs of varying complexity, from simple office renovations to complex structural modifications that enable new use cases. Our endeavors often generate returns exceeding 100% per year with relatively short holding periods.

Our role in repositioning programs varies depending on your needs. We can assist with opportunity analysis, strategic planning and project financing.

Asset Management

Owning investment property is a business. And like any business it requires continuous management and periodic course correction. Our strategic asset management service enables investors and owners of income property to relax and let us monitor the asset to maximize its financial performance. Services include:

  • Development and execution of a property specific strategic plan, including financing strategies.
  • Management of all administrative duties.
  • Continuous monitoring of financial performance against projections.
  • Preparation of an annual “hold or sell” analysis.
  • Execution of acquisition, disposition or re-investment when appropriate.

Centerra Capital offers brokerage services to our in-house clients. Whether you are a buyer, seller, borrower, investor or user, we work hard to maximize proceeds, minimize taxes and achieve optimal accounting treatments.

LEASING

We are strongly networked in our markets, ensuring that your units receive superior exposure with the right businesses and relocation services. Our leasing agents apply a rigorous screening process to ensure lessees are not only qualified, but a good long-term business fit for your property.
